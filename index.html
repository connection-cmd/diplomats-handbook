How it works

You are handed a situation. You choose how you would reply. Then all three responses are revealed — the best tack, the honourable-with-caveats, and the misstep — each with a candid critique.

"At a dinner party, someone you have just met asks: so how much do you make? The table goes quiet."

The best tack"Enough to keep me in good wine and bad opinions. What about you — what do you do?" — deflects with warmth, hands the question back.
Honourable, with caveats"I'd rather not say, if that's alright." — honest and clean, though it can leave a small chill on the table.
The misstep"That's a rude question." — true, perhaps, but it punishes the room for one person's slip.
