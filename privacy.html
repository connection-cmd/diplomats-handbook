Privacy Policy

Effective date: 30 May 2026 · Last updated: 30 May 2026

This privacy policy explains how The Diplomat's Handbook (the "App") handles information. The App is designed to work entirely on your device: no account is required, no personal information is requested, and no analytics or tracking are used. This policy describes that in detail so you can verify it.

1. Who we are

The App is published by AlHadi Development ("we", "us", or "our"). You can reach us at support@alhadidevelopment.com.

2. Information we collect

None. The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information about you. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any other contact details.
  • Your location, device identifiers, advertising IDs, or hardware information.
  • Any usage analytics, behavioural metrics, or crash reports.
  • Any payment information — subscription billing is handled exclusively by Google Play (see Section 5).
  • Photos, contacts, calendars, files, or any data outside the App itself.

You may, if you wish, enter a first name in the App so that it can address you by it ("Welcome back, your name"). That name is stored only on your device. It is never transmitted to us or to any third party.

3. Information stored on your device

To remember your progress between sessions, the App stores the following information locally on your device using your browser/operating-system's standard on-device storage (the WebView's local storage):

  • Your answers to the four-question intent quiz used to personalise the journey order.
  • Your current rank, reputation, daily streak, and which scenarios you have completed.
  • Any reflections you choose to write after a lesson.
  • Your narration preferences (speed, voice, auto-play) and whether you have agreed to local reminder notifications.

This information never leaves your device. It is not synchronised to any server. Uninstalling the App deletes all of it.

4. Local reminder notifications

If you grant the optional notification permission, the App schedules a single, gentle daily reminder using your device's built-in notification system. The reminder is generated locally on your device; no notification content is sent from a server, and we do not know whether or when your device displays it. You may revoke this permission at any time in your device's settings.

5. Subscriptions and payments

If you choose to purchase a subscription, the transaction is processed by Google Play Billing. Google receives your payment details directly; the App and its publisher do not see, store, or process card numbers or other payment information. Your purchase history is governed by Google's privacy policy.

The App stores a single flag locally indicating that you are entitled to premium content, plus the product ID you purchased, so that the paywall stays unlocked offline. This flag is verified against Google Play whenever the App is online.

6. Third-party services

The App uses the following third-party platform services:

  • Google Play Billing — for subscription purchases (see Section 5).
  • Your device's text-to-speech engine (e.g. Google's TTS on Android) — for reading lessons aloud. Lesson text is sent to the on-device engine only; no audio data leaves your device.

The App does not use any advertising networks, analytics services, crash reporting services, attribution SDKs, or social-login providers.

7. Children's privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under the age of 13. Because the App does not collect any personal information, it does not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used the App and that we have somehow received personal information about them, please contact us so we can confirm that no such information exists in our possession.

8. Security

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no server-side dataset to protect. Information stored on your device is protected by your device's own security model (lock screen, encryption, etc.). Subscription transactions transmitted to Google Play are encrypted in transit by Google.

9. Your rights and choices

  • Erase your data. All App data is stored on your device. To delete it, either use the "Begin anew" reset inside the App's profile screen, or uninstall the App. There is no server-side data to delete.
  • Withdraw notification consent. Revoke the notification permission in your device settings at any time. The App will continue to work normally.
  • Cancel a subscription. You may cancel anytime via Google Play (Subscriptions). Cancellation prevents future renewals; access continues to the end of the current billing period.
  • Statutory rights. Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as the EU GDPR, the UK GDPR, the California CCPA/CPRA, or other privacy laws. Because the App does not collect personal data, most of these rights are satisfied by design; if you have a specific request, please contact us.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy if the App's behaviour changes (for example, if a future feature requires data collection). When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top and, for material changes, surface a notice within the App. The current policy is always the version at this URL.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy or our privacy practices can be sent to support@alhadidevelopment.com. We will reply within a reasonable time.
